What We Do

Three pillars of partnership designed to fit your business reality.

1

Business Analysis & AI Discovery

Before execution, we evaluate.

This phase includes:

  • Company profile and executive summary
  • Revenue and margin analysis
  • Customer avatar and buying journey
  • Lead-to-close process review
  • Marketing budget feasibility
  • AI and automation opportunities

The outcome is clarity. On what to automate. On what to measure. On whether a partnership makes sense.

2

End-to-End Marketing Systems

Once the foundation is sound, we build and operate marketing systems end to end.

Capabilities include:

  • Brand positioning and messaging
  • Website design and conversion optimization
  • Graphic design and video production
  • Paid advertising (search and social)
  • Email and lifecycle campaigns
  • Social media management
  • Lead qualification and handoff systems

Marketing does not stop at traffic. It ends when a qualified opportunity is delivered to the business.

3

Marketing for Equity (Selective)

Some businesses have strong fundamentals but limited capital.

For a small number of companies, we offer Marketing for Equity.

This includes:

  • Operating the marketing function
  • Long-term partnership alignment
  • Equity and/or profit participation

This path requires:

  • An application
  • A formal evaluation
  • Clear product-market fit
  • Shared long-term incentives

We only partner where we believe deeply in the outcome.
